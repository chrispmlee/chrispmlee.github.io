Team: Jonathan K. Lee, Quinn Huelsbeck Christopher P. Lee

Introduction

This is a project that sprung up Mid-November 2017. I was watching the Stanford Design Impact information session, and one of the speakers noted that we can always start things now to pursue our passions. Mental health has always been an important piece in my life, and as my Grandmother combats anxiety and alzheimers I've been looking for solutions. Moment blankets was born.

I've always had the entreprenurial spirit and have worked on numerous side projects, trying to bring them into fruition. I thought why not now, with this solution that has brought so much peace to my Grandma? Moment blankets has been an opportunity to put a lot of my different skills to the test. Moment blankets is in the development stage, and this page will be filled out shortly with our process, plan, and reserach.
